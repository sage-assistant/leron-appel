# BUILD BRIEF: THE APPEL DYNASTY — A GAME OF SPARES

## What You're Building
A single `index.html` file (inline CSS + JS, no external deps except Google Fonts) that is a fake HBO prestige drama landing page for "The Appel Dynasty: A Game of Spares." This is a comedy gift site for Leron Appel. The humor comes from treating a Johannesburg scrapyard like it's Game of Thrones. Played COMPLETELY straight. The more seriously it's played, the funnier it is.

## Reference
Study `reference-example.html` in this directory. Match that level of quality, polish, interactivity, and comedy density. That's the gold standard.

## The Person: Leron Appel
- Third-generation owner of **Lou Appel's Auto Spares**, Selby/Booysens Road, Johannesburg
- Family dynasty spanning ~90 years:
  - **Max Appel** (great-grandfather): Blacksmith who bought the land
  - **Lou Appel** (grandfather): Started the scrapyard business. Originally called "Lou Appel's Motor Graveyard" with a SKULL AND CROSSBONES logo. Died February 1982.
  - **Raymond Appel** (father): Formed Lou Appel's Auto Spares TVL (PTY) Ltd in January 1983. Ran it until 2014.
  - **Leron Appel** (current heir): Raymond sold the business to him in 2014. Third generation.
- The business: "Home of a Million Spares" — they buy damaged/non-running vehicles, strip them, sell used parts (engines, gearboxes, body parts). Located at 233 Booysens Road, corner Webber Street, Selby, Johannesburg.
- Main brands they stock parts for: wide range of vehicle makes and models
- Website: louappels.co.za
- Facebook: Lou Appel's Auto Spares (2,200+ likes)

## Design Rules (FOLLOW STRICTLY)

### Typography
Use Google Fonts only. Suggested pairing:
- Headlines: Something dramatic/serif (Playfair Display, Cinzel, or similar)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (Inter, Manrope, or similar)

### Colors — HBO Dark Cinematic
- Background: Never pure black. Use #0A0A0A or #0C0C0E
- Surface: #121214 or #141414
- Primary accent: Deep crimson/blood red (like HBO drama)
- Secondary accent: Gold (for "prestige" elements)
- Text: #F5F5F7 (off-white)
- Muted text: #86868B

### Spacing
- 4px base grid
- Section padding: minimum 80px top/bottom
- Generous whitespace everywhere
- Container max-width: 1200px

### Animations
- Scroll-triggered reveals (IntersectionObserver)
- Smooth hover effects
- Typing effects where appropriate
- Counter/ticker animations
- Use cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1) for reveals
- Duration: 300ms for hovers, 500ms for reveals, 800ms for hero load

### Critical Rules
- **NO EMOJIS anywhere on the site.** Use CSS icons, SVG icons, or Unicode symbols instead. This is non-negotiable.
- **NO DASHES (—, –, -) in copy text.** Use commas, periods, or restructure sentences instead. This is non-negotiable. Dashes in CSS/code are fine, just not in visible text content.
- Single HTML file with all CSS and JS inline
- Mobile responsive (test at 768px and 480px breakpoints)
- At least 3 interactive elements
- At least 2 hidden easter eggs (e.g., Konami code, click counters, console messages)
- The humor comes from ABSURD ELEVATION played completely straight. Never mock. Make Leron feel like a legend.
- Every single piece of text should be comedy. No filler. No generic lines.

### Shadows
- Subtle: 0 1px 2px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)
- Medium: 0 4px 6px -1px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)
- Large: 0 10px 15px -3px rgba(0,0,0,0.1)
- Use glow effects for dramatic elements: box-shadow with accent colors

### Border Radius
- Cards: 16px-24px
- Buttons: 100px (pill) or 12px
- Small elements: 8px
